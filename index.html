About Hampi

Once upon a time, Hampi was one of the thriving, largest and richest cities of erstwhile Vijanagar Empire, besides being the seat of power of the Empire itself. It also was and still is an important spiritual center and there are a a number of religious architectures and temples, richness in art and architecture.
